Program

It starts before you are here, and never stops.

Pre-Program

~80 hours

You will receive carefully curated material to study before joining the program. This covers the foundations in Mathematics (Linear Algebra, Calculus, Probability and Statistics), Computer Science (Machine Learning and Deep Learning) and Software Engineering (Python, Git, TensorFlow, PyTorch).



Foundation

2 Weeks

An intense hands on review of foundational concepts in machine learning including supervised and unsupervised methods and state of the art deep learning research. You will develope and present your first real world application in life science.



Advanced Topics

3 Weeks

Week 1: Overview of various life science problems, trends and opportunities. Invited talks by ML practitioners from leading bio-medical & pharma companies & research organization including Genentech, Illumina, Celgene, Roche, UCSF, Stanford, Berkeley, etc.

Week 2,3: Coursework on life science topics, office hours as well as development and presentation of the second life science project.



Final Project

3 Weeks

Week 1: You will pair with our industrial & academic partners to define their final project.

Week 2,3: Project implementation. Often these projects are performed on site at the facility of the chosen industrial partner, although access to the Data For Life offices and in-house ML experts will be available throughout the duration of the project.



Demo Day

2 Days

Two days of networking, interviews and presentations.



Post-Program

Warm reccommendation to our industry partners to get a job. You will also continue to have access to all members of Data for Life network, events and partners.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the program cost?

The registration fee for the whole 8 weeks is $4000.

How can I apply for scholarships?

Yes, there are several scholarships available which you can apply for during your application process. Some scholarship cover registation fee and some cover move and accomdation expenses.

Can I participate remotely?

No, the program will require you to participate fulltime at our office in San Francisco.

What does the registration fee cover?

It covers all the course related material, a dedicated desk in our SF office, snacks and coffee.

Do I need a PhD?

No, but you need to demostrate strong academic and research background.

Is the registration fee refundable?

You can cancel your participation and get a full refund until 1 month before the program start date. After that the enrolment is final and registration fee is not refundable.
